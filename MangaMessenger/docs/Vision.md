# Manga Messenger Vision

## Our Mission

Manga Messenger is more than just another messaging application.

Our goal is to create a messaging experience that feels expressive, immersive, and unique by combining modern communication with the visual storytelling style of manga.

---

## Core Principles

### 🎨 Expressive Communication

Messages should communicate emotion, not just words.

Users will be able to choose different manga-inspired speech bubbles and emotional styles that match how they feel.

---

### ⚡ Beautiful User Experience

Every interaction should feel smooth and polished.

Animations should enhance the experience without becoming distracting.

---

### 📱 Cross-Platform

Manga Messenger should work on:

- Desktop
- Mobile phones
- Tablets
- Progressive Web App (PWA)

Future versions may also include native desktop and mobile applications.

---

### ☁️ Real-Time Communication

Users should be able to communicate instantly through:

- Text messages
- Voice calls
- Video calls
- Media sharing

---

### 🎭 Emotion Bubble Engine

One of Manga Messenger's signature features will allow users to choose speech bubble styles inspired by manga emotions, such as:

- Normal
- Happy
- Excited
- Angry
- Sad
- Shocked
- Nervous
- Love
- Whisper
- Thinking

These emotions will change the appearance of message bubbles while keeping conversations readable and fun.

---

## Long-Term Goal

Build a messaging platform that feels creative, expressive, and instantly recognizable while remaining fast, reliable, and easy to use.
